Description

Kutralam, also known as Courtallam, is a charming town located in the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is renowned for its picturesque waterfalls, which are a major tourist attraction. The town is situated in the Western Ghats and is known for its lush greenery and pleasant climate. The main highlight of Kutralam is its numerous waterfalls, each with its unique name and characteristics. The most famous among them include the Main Falls, Five Falls, Old Courtallam Falls, Tiger Falls, and many others. These waterfalls are formed by the Chittar River and its tributaries as they cascade down the hills, creating a breathtaking natural spectacle. Visitors flock to Kutralam to enjoy the therapeutic and rejuvenating properties of the mineral-rich water from these falls, which is believed to have medicinal benefits. The falls are surrounded by lush forests, providing a serene and refreshing environment for tourists.
